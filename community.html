Purpose

Some immigrants come to the United States and feel left out due to the different culture and language surrounding them. Additionally, the people in the community do not always go out of their way to welcome immigrants due to the stigma that has been associated with them by the current government. Our solution is to offer a map with pinned locations where immigrants can find community centers based on their ethnicity in order to make them feel more comfortable in the new environment.
