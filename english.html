Purpose

Immigrants come to the United States in search of a better life. However, often they are unable to obtain the jobs they are capable of because of the language barrier. According to the Public Policy Institute of California, 28% of immigrants in the United States live in households where no one speaks English. This isolation from English speakers makes it harder for immigrants to grow accustomed to the English language, making it even more difficult to assimilate into the new community. Many immigrants come to the United States with the intention of learning English. Unfortunately, more often than not, they end up being in classes with people from different ethnicities, making it even more challenging for them to learn the new language. Our solution is to have a part of our website dedicated to classes where immigrants can learn English in an environment where the other people around them speak the same native language, enabling them to reach out to their peers for help and motivate each other for help
